The Heroes and Villains of Adventure Time!

Character Information Abilities

Finn Mertens

 Finn is a brave, adventurous human boy with a deep sense of justice and a love for heroism. Wearing his iconic bear hat, he embarks on countless quests, helping those in need across the Land of Ooo. Though he’s energetic and a little reckless, he has a big heart and a loyal nature.
  • Superhuman Strength
  • Swordsmanship Skills
  • Martial Arts
  • Creativity
  • Musical Skills

Jacob "Jake" Sr.

 Finn's best friend and adoptive brother, Jake is a magical dog with shape-shifting powers. Laid-back and fun-loving, he often takes a more relaxed approach to their adventures but always supports Finn. He also loves cooking and playing music and has a penchant for joking around and offering words of wisdom when Finn needs them.
  • Body Manipulation
  • Martial Arts
  • High Sense of Smell
  • Creativity
  • Musical Skills

Princess Bonnibel "Bonnie" Bubblegum

 The ruler of the Candy Kingdom, Princess Bubblegum is a smart, scientific, and sometimes strict leader who often juggles diplomacy and invention. While she’s typically compassionate and cares for her subjects, she can be cold and calculating when needed, showing her dedication to the kingdom’s safety above all else.
  • High Intelligence
  • Leadership Skills
  • Bilinguality
  • Martial Arts
  • Candy Body/Powers

Marceline Abadeer

 A thousand-year-old vampire queen with a passion for rock music, Marceline is rebellious, carefree, and fiercely independent. With her bass guitar made from her family’s battle axe, she roams the Land of Ooo, sometimes as an ally and other times as a lone wanderer. Her complex personality often hides a sensitive and lonely side.
  • Demonic/Vampire Powers
  • Immortality
  • Martial Arts
  • Fire Control
  • Musical Skills

Ice King

 Once a kind scholar named Simon, the Ice King has become an eccentric, lonely old wizard driven by his obsession to find a princess to marry. His magical crown grants him ice powers but has distorted his mind, making him unpredictable. Beneath his goofy and villainous exterior, remnants of his former compassionate self sometimes emerge.
  • Ice Powers
  • Flying
  • Martial Arts
  • Heightened Senses
  • Wizard Eyes
